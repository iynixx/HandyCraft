    About Handy Craft

    The Handy Craft Collective

    Handy Craft was born from a passion for sustainable, slow craft and the desire to support local Malaysian artisans. We aim to be the premier online destination for unique, high-quality crochet and handmade goods.

    Mission | Vision | Values

    Mission

    We aim to be the premier online destination for unique, high-quality crochet and handmade goods, offering an alternative to mass-produced items.

    Vision

    To foster a thriving community where creativity meets conscious consumption, ensuring every purchase tells a story and contributes positively to the artisan economy.

    Values

    • Quality: Every item is thoroughly inspected for superior craftsmanship.
    • Sustainability: We prioritize eco-friendly materials and ethical production.
    • Community: We are committed to empowering local makers.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How long does shipping take?

    Since all items are handmade, processing time can take 3-5 business days. Shipping within Malaysia usually takes an additional 2-3 days.

    What is your return policy?

    We accept returns on damaged items within 7 days of delivery. Due to the unique nature of handmade goods, we do not offer returns for change of mind.

    Are the products machine washable?

    Most items require delicate hand washing or spot cleaning. Please refer to the specific care instructions provided with your product.

    Do you accept custom commissions?

    Yes. Many of our artisans love creating custom pieces. Please use the contact form to provide details, and we will get back to you with a quote.

    How do I track my order?

    Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email containing your tracking number and a link to the courier portal.

    Is international shipping available?

    Currently, we primarily ship within Malaysia. However, we can arrange international shipping on a case-by-case basis. Contact us for an estimate.
