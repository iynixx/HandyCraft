About Handy Craft

Sustainable Slow Craft • Malaysian Heritage • Conscious Living

The Handy Craft Collective

Handy Craft was born from a deep-rooted passion for sustainable, slow craft and a desire to uplift the incredible talent within the Malaysian artisan community. In a world of fast fashion and mass-produced goods, we serve as a sanctuary for those who value the time, heart, and soul poured into every stitch. We are more than just a marketplace; we are a bridge between traditional heritage and modern lifestyle.

Mission | Vision | Values

Mission

Our mission is to empower local Malaysian artisans by providing a professional platform that celebrates their unique craftsmanship. We are committed to supporting **United Nations SDG 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth)**, striving to provide sustainable livelihoods while educating consumers on the value of "slow craft." We offer high-quality, ethically-made alternatives that challenge the environmental impact of industrial mass production, keeping traditional arts alive for future generations.

Vision

To become Malaysia’s leading ecosystem for the artisan economy, where creativity meets conscious consumption. We envision a future where every household cherishes at least one handmade item that tells a story—fostering a society that chooses quality over quantity and local impact over global industrialization. We aim to inspire a movement where the human connection behind a product is valued as much as the product itself.

Values

  • Uncompromising Quality: We don't just sell goods; we curate art. Every item undergoes a rigorous inspection to ensure superior durability and craftsmanship.
  • Radical Sustainability: We prioritize an "Earth-First" approach, favoring natural fibers and zero-waste production methods to minimize our carbon footprint.
  • Artisan Empowerment (SDG 8): We believe in fair wages and creative dignity. By aligning with **SDG 8**, we prioritize partnerships with rural makers and stay-at-home parents to provide sustainable income and foster inclusive economic growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Since all items are handmade, processing time can take 3-5 business days. Shipping within Malaysia usually takes an additional 2-3 days.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns on damaged items within 7 days of delivery. Due to the unique nature of handmade goods, we do not offer returns for change of mind.

Are the products machine washable?

Most items require delicate hand washing or spot cleaning. Please refer to the specific care instructions provided with your product.

Do you accept custom commissions?

Yes. Many of our artisans love creating custom pieces. Please use the contact form to provide details, and we will get back to you with a quote.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email containing your tracking number and a link to the courier portal.

Is international shipping available?

Currently, we primarily ship within Malaysia. However, we can arrange international shipping on a case-by-case basis. Contact us for an estimate.
