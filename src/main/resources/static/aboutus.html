About Handy Craft

Our Story: The Handy Craft Collective

Handy Craft was born from a passion for sustainable, slow craft and the desire to support local Malaysian artisans. We aim to be the premier online destination for unique, high-quality crochet and handmade goods.

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Mission

We aim to be the premier online destination for unique, high-quality crochet and handmade goods, offering an alternative to mass-produced items.

Vision

To foster a thriving community where creativity meets conscious consumption, ensuring every purchase tells a story and contributes positively to the artisan economy.

Values

  • Quality: Every item is thoroughly inspected for superior craftsmanship.
  • Sustainability: We prioritize eco-friendly materials and ethical production methods.
  • Community: We are committed to empowering local makers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does shipping take?

A: Since all items are handmade, processing time can take 3-5 business days. Shipping within Malaysia usually takes an additional 2-3 days.

Q: What is your return policy?

A: We accept returns on damaged items within 7 days of delivery. Due to the unique nature of handmade goods, we do not offer returns for change of mind.

Q: Are the products machine washable?

A: Most items require delicate hand washing or spot cleaning. Please refer to the specific care instructions provided with your product.
