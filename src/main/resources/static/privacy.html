Privacy Policy

Your Data Security & Artisan Trust

Effective Date: December 12, 2025

At Handy Craft, we are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) 2010. This policy outlines our commitment to transparency and ethical data management as we bridge the gap between Malaysian artisans and conscious consumers.

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect information necessary to provide a premium e-commerce experience. This includes:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and encrypted passwords.
  • Transaction Data: Shipping addresses and order history.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses and browser cookies to improve site performance and security.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

Your information is processed to fulfill our commitment to SDG 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth) by:

  • Facilitating secure transactions and delivery of handmade goods.
  • Managing custom commissions and artisan-to-customer communication.
  • Providing updates on our Malaysian artisan community and sustainability initiatives.

3. Artisan & Third-Party Disclosure

To ensure the integrity of "slow craft," we share necessary shipping details with the specific local artisan fulfilling your order. All partners are contractually bound to maintain confidentiality and are prohibited from using your data for marketing or unauthorized purposes. We never sell your personal information to third-party advertisers.

4. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard security protocols to safeguard your data. In compliance with Malaysian tax laws and quality assurance standards, purchase records are retained for **five years**. This allows us to track material inventory and provide long-term support for your artisanal products.

5. Your Rights

Under the PDPA, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights or for any inquiries regarding your privacy, please contact us at support@handycraft.my.
