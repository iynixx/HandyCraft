Handy Craft Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 12, 2025

Your privacy is important to Handy Craft. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in compliance with Malaysian data protection principles.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and shipping address when you make a purchase or sign up for an account. We also collect basic usage data like IP address and browser type for security and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to process transactions, deliver products, communicate order updates (especially custom order status), and improve our website experience. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Artisan Data Sharing

When you place an order, we may share only necessary shipping and contact details with the specific local artisan creating your product to ensure timely delivery and, in some cases, personalized communication regarding the craft. Artisans are contractually bound to use this information only for the purpose of fulfilling your order.

4. Data Retention and Security

We retain your account and purchase history for five years for tax and quality assurance purposes, especially relating to the materials and inventory used by our local Malaysian artisans. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information.
